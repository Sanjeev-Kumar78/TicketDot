**TicketDot** is a blockchain-verified ticket booking platform built on Polkadot that eliminates ticket fraud and scalping by representing each ticket as a unique NFT. Event organizers can create events on-chain, and attendees receive cryptographically secure, transferable tickets that prove authenticity and ownership.

## ðŸ’¡ Problem Statement

Traditional ticketing platforms face critical issues:

- **Ticket Fraud**: Counterfeit tickets cost the industry billions annually
- **Scalping & Price Manipulation**: Bots buy tickets in bulk, reselling at inflated prices
- **Lack of Transparency**: No verifiable proof of ticket authenticity
- **Centralized Control**: Platform fees are high, and users don't truly own their tickets
